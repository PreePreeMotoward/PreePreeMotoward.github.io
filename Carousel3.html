The Araw ng Dabaw Celebration and Garbo sa Dabaw

by: SR Pasaporte

The Araw ng Dabaw celebration is one of the most highly anticipated events in Davao City, Philippines. It is a celebration of the city’s founding and showcases the rich culture and heritage of the Davaoeños. As part of the 86th Araw ng Dabaw celebration, the Pasidungog 2023: Garbo sa Dabaw is held to recognize the outstanding individuals, civic groups, and companies that have contributed to the city’s growth and development.

One aspect of this recognition is the giving of certificates and plaques to companies that have demonstrated an exceptional level of participation and engagement in the Public Employment Service Office’s (PESO) activities. These companies invest time, resources, and effort to ensure that the job fairs are successful. By giving them a plaque of recognition, they are acknowledged for their hard work and contribution to the success of various activities.

At the awarding ceremony, the plaques were presented to the representatives of the companies, and a brief ceremony was held to celebrate the occasion. The event was attended by local officials, representatives of participating companies, and other stakeholders who witnessed the recognition. Representatives from these companies received the award given by the City Government of Davao Department Heads and Chiefs led by the City Administrator, Atty. Mark H. Layog, Vice Mayor Jay Melchor B. Quitain Jr. with PESO’s Supervising Labor and Employment Officer Maam Lilibeth D. Pantinople.

In conclusion, the giving of certificates and plaques through The Pasidungog 2023: Garbo sa Dabaw during the 86th Araw ng Dabaw is an important aspect of recognizing their contribution. It is a testament to the commitment of these companies to their community and corporate social responsibility, their dedication to not only fulfilling their staffing needs but also contributing to the development of their community. These companies have recognized the value of investing in their human capital and have shown their dedication to promoting job opportunities for all.

By continuing to participate in job fairs, these companies demonstrate their resilience and commitment to helping their community even during challenging times.
