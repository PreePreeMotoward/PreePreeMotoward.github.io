HAVE YOUR VACANCIES POSTED!

To post your vacancies, please send us updated copies of the following documents:

  1. Business Permit
  2. BIR 2303
  3. SEC / DTI Certificate
  4. List of vacancies in MS Word following this format:

<number of vacancies><position title><qualifications and area of assignment>

Please include: contact person and designation, contact details (landline/mobile numbers, email address)

Send them to: peso@davaocity.gov.ph
